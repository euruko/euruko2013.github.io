Description

EuRuKo is a European annual conference, organised each year in a different European city. The Athens Ruby community is organising the 11th EuRuKo conference in Athens, on the 28th and 29th of June 2013.

The Site and the Service operate exclusively in order to a) provide information to the public regarding the EuRuKo 2013 conference, on the 28 - 29 of June, b) sell tickets, via a third party ticketing management company.
